Expert insights for real-world solutions: Welcome to the Institute of Applied Thought Leadership

Ben Brandwood · 30 December 2022

Welcome to the Institute of Applied Thought Leadership! We are a group of experts from a variety of fields, including education, universities, retail, and technology, based in Winchester, UK. Our aim is to bring together diverse perspectives and expertise to tackle the complex challenges facing society today.

At the Institute of Applied Thought Leadership, we believe that addressing these challenges requires a multi-disciplinary approach. By bringing together a diverse group of experts, we can draw on a wide range of knowledge and experience to generate innovative solutions and insights.

Our focus is on applied thought leadership — taking the latest research and ideas and turning them into practical, actionable solutions that can be implemented in the real world. We aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry, and to make a real difference in the world.

Some of the areas we are focusing on include:

  • Education: Our experts in education are working to develop new approaches to teaching and learning that can help students achieve their full potential.
  • Universities: We are exploring ways to enhance the university experience and make higher education more accessible and affordable.
  • Retail: Our retail experts are looking at ways to improve the customer experience and drive innovation in the industry.
  • Technology: Our technology experts are at the forefront of developing new technologies and exploring their potential uses and applications.

We are excited to be launching the Institute of Applied Thought Leadership and we look forward to working with partners and stakeholders to make a positive impact on society. If you are interested in collaborating with us or learning more about our work, please do not hesitate to get in touch.
