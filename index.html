Who We Are

The Institute of Applied Thought Leadership is a UK-based organisation dedicated to helping individuals and organisations achieve their potential through the development of effective leadership strategies.

We bring together a team of leadership experts, educators, and practitioners who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and expertise. Our aim is to empower the next generation of leaders and make a positive impact in the world.
